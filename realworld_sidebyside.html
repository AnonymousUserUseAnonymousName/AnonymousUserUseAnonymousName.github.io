Zero-Shot Sim-to-Real Transfer of PPO-UrbanVerse Policies in Urban and Campus Environments

We present side-by-side comparisons of the zero-shot sim-to-real transfer of PPO-UrbanVerse policies in urban and campus environments across two robot platforms. All videos are recorded from our actual real-world experiments and correspond directly to the results reported in the paper.
