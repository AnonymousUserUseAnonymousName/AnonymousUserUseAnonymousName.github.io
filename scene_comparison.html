UrbanVerse vs. Urban-Sim

A Comparison Between UrbanVerse’s Data-Driven Real-to-Sim Scenes (left) and Procedurally Generated (PG) Urban-Sim Scenes (right)

As shown below, PG scenes rely on hard-coded rules and templates, typically placing objects along sidewalks or roads at fixed intervals. As a result, they often fail to capture the diversity and richness of real-world environments.

In contrast, UrbanVerse’s data-driven real-to-sim approach produces scenes that are faithfully grounded in real-world layouts extracted from city-tour videos.
