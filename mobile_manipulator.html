Beyond Navigation: Mobile Manipulation in UrbanVerse Scenes

We present two demonstrations of a mobile manipulation task, consisting of navigating to a target and picking up an object, highlighting UrbanVerse’s general-purpose support for urban embodied AI tasks beyond navigation-only scenarios.
